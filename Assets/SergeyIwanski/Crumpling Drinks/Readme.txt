Thank you for having purchased our asset!

This package contains can and bottle models with their the crumpled copies.

The bottles have a Crumple component. In the Crumpled property, you can choose a crumpled can or bottle model.
You do not need to customize the materials of crumpled models, just paste them into the Crumpled property, 
everything else is done by the script itself.
In the "Crumple" script, you can change the OnMouseDown() method to any other on whatever you want.

All the textures in the PSD format, you can easily replace the label and color. 
The original resolution is 1024 x 1204.
